The Architecture

Input

Sensors, Satellite & Transboundary Data

Processing

LSTM Machine Learning Engine

Output

5-Level Risk Classification

Action

SMS Broadcast & Anticipatory Cash (bKash)

Why SMS?

In rural Bangladesh, internet is inconsistent. But 114 million people have mobile subscriptions. SMS works on 2G. It is the most resilient last-mile channel.

Anticipatory Finance

When the model predicts Level 4 risk, mobile money transfers are automatically triggered to registered households before the flood peaks.

11 / 15

BondhuHaat

A digital flood relief coordination platform

Needs Mapping

Register vulnerable households 72 hours before flood. Linked to BD-Alert predictions.

Direct Transfer

Send money via bKash directly to families. No middlemen. No cash handled.

Supply Matching

Match bulk donors with verified NGOs to prevent oversupply and shortages.

Open Dashboard

Public real-time dashboard of total funds transferred and delivery status.
